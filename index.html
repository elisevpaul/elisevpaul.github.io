Every year the amount of time we spend in traffic is increasing. At the same time the cost of vehicle ownership continues to grow.

This increase is hard to notice day to day but looking at the data directly on an annual basis helps to see this growth more clearly.

These costs include variable costs such as fuel and maintenance.

Fixed costs are also increasing, these include things like insurance, depreciation, cost of financing or leasing and sometimes storage.

Time spent in traffic not only increases these costs but also reduces the amount of time away from home.
